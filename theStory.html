All Stories Have a Beginning

Born and raised in Brooklyn, New York, and always having a fascination for music and creativity, I wanted to pursue something combining music and technology, thus I thought to pursue NYU's music technology program. Of course, upon being advised and encouraged that pursuing computer science would be a better choice giving me more career options, I explored and was accepted into the Seidenberg School of Computer Science and Information Systems at Pace University, NYC, where I am currently a sophomore pursuing a combined degree of a Bachelors of Arts in Computer Science and Masters of Science in Information Technology. After completing my first year and halfway through my second, I discovered I had an interest in 3D modeling, animation, and computer graphics and I considered it to be a possible career path.
